Arcane Satin Lined Beanies

Arcane Satin Lined Beanies: This beanie has satin line material inside which is a soft and lustrous fabric with a shiny front. Satin lined also a slick material that allows your hair to move across its surface with minimal friction. Minimal friction = minimal breakage, which means a satin lining can help your hair stay healthy and strong.
