Arcane Flared Sweatpants

Arcane Sweatpants: These pants have white strips on the side with 2 custom designs. The first one is a ripped/destroyed star I made and the second one is just a graffiti design of my brand name called "Arcane". This clothing piece is made out of cotton with the white design's heat press onto the pants and 2 sides pockets, 2 back pocket, and drawstrings. The special part about these sweats is that, at the bottom they flare outwards.
